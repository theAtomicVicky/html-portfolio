Best movies according to Vicky

My top 3 movies of all time:

Once upon a time in the west

Excellent movie, with a great soundtrack. The main character's revenge story is gut-clenching and Sergio Leone's music is awesome.

The Royal Tenembaums

Wes Anderson's movies are simply beautiful. This one I particulary like for reasons I have yet to unravel, maybe is the final realization that love between people who have hurt each other so much is greater than what we can either explain or understand.

Ghost In the Shell

I love animated movies, and really like anime. Old style anime has a great tone to it, and the animation feels just right. Not only that, but this one is a movie that forces us to question what does it mean to be human, and how far can we strecch the concept of it.
